Why Should I Join A Fraternity?

Fratenity life is one of the most life-changing and rewarding experience offered to gentlemen in college. With a tradition of more than 180 years, fraternities strive for a high level of excellence and success. Through individual chapter academic enrichment and community service programs, fraternity men have played an influential role on their campus, in their surrounding area, and throughout the country. Many fraternity men have gone on to lead success careers, manage major companies, guide international philathropic foundations, and hold high offices in the government.
